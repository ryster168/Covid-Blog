Life in March, 2021

How Covid affected my school

I heard about Covid on the TV but only kept it in the back of my mind. It was nearing the end of school when I was told that we would be doing online for around three weeks because of covid.

When we never came back...

April, 2021

After the three weeks was over I heard that Covid was ramping up. I also knew we were probably never come back until the whole pandemic was over. What I was expecting was true we would have to finish the rest of the semester "ONLINE"

Scary Summer

During summertime which was supposed to be full of vacations and fun. It was instead filled with me just sitting on a couch like a potato, watching the cases and severity of Covid increase dramatically.

Delta Strain

When Delta hit I was even more concerned. Especially when people are indoors partying without masks.

The Spike

I watched the Covid cases spike as soon as the Delta variant started, then all of a sudden Delta+ came, which is stronger at defending itself from immunity.
